The morning sun filtered through the dense canopy of trees,
casting flickering patterns of gold across the forest floor.
A gentle breeze carried the crisp, clean scent of damp earth and blooming wildflowers, whispering softly through the pine needles.
Somewhere in the distance, a solitary bird began its morning song, its clear notes echoing through the quiet woods.
It was a brief, untouched moment where time seemed to slow down, offering a perfect pause before the rush of the day began.
